May 11, 2009

I received a Visiting Fellow award from National Institute of Health (NIH) for postdoctoral research training at National Cancer Institute (NCI). My research interests include the molecular mechanisms of tumorigenesis and gene regulation during development.

  • Long non-coding RNA (LncRNA) in muscle development
  • Cis-regulatory elements of Nautilus gene

May 9, 2009

I arrived in the U.S. with my husband. I will work at NIH/NCI as a researcher while he will pursue some changes in life and career. You are welcome to the gallery Impressions of America and our personal blog Not Dreams.
